# ArcGesture Puzzle Games User Guide

This guide describes how to play the two gesture-controlled puzzle games integrated into the ArcGesture HUD workspace: **Puzzle Cam Photobooth** and **Puzzle Hands Decryption**.

---

## 📸 Game 1: Puzzle Cam Photobooth

Puzzle Cam is a gesture-controlled photobooth and slice-puzzle game. 

### 🖐️ Hand Gesture Controls
*   **Framing Gesture (Activate Screen)**: Hold both hands up, index fingers pointing up, and thumbs pointing toward each other. The photobooth camera frame box will appear.
*   **Double-Hand Pinch (Start Countdown)**: Pinch your index finger and thumb on both hands inside the frame. This triggers a 3-second countdown.
*   **Single-Hand Pinch (Drag & Drop)**: Hover your index finger over a puzzle piece and pinch to drag it. Release the pinch near its target cell to snap it into place.
*   **Fist (Shatter / Reset)**: When the puzzle is complete, hold a fist with either hand for 12 frames. This shatters the solved image into physics fragments and saves the photo.
*   **Thumbs Up (Record Gameplay)**: Hold a thumbs-up gesture with either hand to automatically record 5 seconds of canvas gameplay and download it as a WebM file.

### 🎮 Gameplay Flow
1.  **Frame the Shot**: Adjust the size of the camera frame by moving your hands closer together or farther apart.
2.  **Snap the Photo**: Pinch both hands to start the countdown. A flash effect will fire, rendering the image as a 3x3 grid of scrambled black-and-white pieces.
3.  **Solve the Puzzle**: Drag pieces with a pinch gesture. Correctly positioned pieces snap to the grid and display in full color.
4.  **Shatter to Polaroid**: Once solved, make a fist to trigger the shatter explosion. The completed photo is compiled into a Polaroid card and added to your gallery.
5.  **Build a Photostrip**: Snap and solve 3 photos to generate a 3-photo vertical Polaroid strip, which you can download as a single PNG image.

## 🧩 Game 2: Puzzle Hands Decryption

Puzzle Hands is a classic sliding decryption grid puzzle that lets you decrypt cyber procedural artwork or live webcam captures using mouse/touch or hand gestures.

### 🖐️ Hand Gesture Controls
*   **Index Finger Target (Cursor)**: Move your index finger to control the cyber reticle on the board.
*   **Pinch (Select / Swap)**:
    *   Hover the cursor over a puzzle tile and pinch (index + thumb) to select it.
    *   Move the cursor to another slot and release the pinch to swap the two tiles.

### 🎮 Gameplay Flow
1.  **Select Game Mode**: Choose **Mouse & Touch** or **AI Hand Tracking**.
2.  **Configure Difficulty**:
    *   **Easy**: 3x3 grid (9 pieces).
    *   **Insane**: 6x6 grid (36 pieces).
3.  **Select Image Matrix**:
    *   **Cyber Procedural Art**: Select from **Sunset** (gradient sunset floor), **Matrix** (binary terminal code rain), or **Neon Grid** (radial wireframe geometry).
    *   **Live Webcam**: Capture your own face or surroundings to slice.
4.  **Decrypt the Matrix**: Swap interlocking tiles until all pieces align with their original coordinates.
5.  **Leaderboard Score**: Your score decays over time. Solve the puzzle quickly to post a high score under your callsign on the decrypted leaderboard.
